My Perspective

On Life
“Twenty years from now you will be more disappointed by the things you didn't do than the ones you did do.” ~Mark Twain

No one is guaranteed their next breath, so capture each moment with passion and gratitude.

On Leadership
"If you always try to be 'the guy,' you'll never be the guy. But if you're helping others be 'the guy,' then you'll always be the guy." ~Bo Short

Surprising opportunities arise when you serve the people you help lead.

On Work
1,000 hours of preparation for one moment of opportunity.

Prepare for big opportunities and you're on the road to success. Don't forget to land the plane—come down from 30,000 ft. and focus on completing the small details.

On Design
If you can't communicate effectively, you can't design effectively.

Learning to understand others from their perspective is the core of communication and design.

On User Experience
Neglect user experience and you'll only ever have one user... you.

Think, track, and act before it's too late.

On eCommerce
Someone with an ugly online store is making more money online than you with no online store.

So start selling online! Tictail.com is the cheapest (free), easiest, and fastest basic solution I know. Magento is the cheapest (free/open source), most robust solution I've found.

On Progress & Technology
If you're not growing, you are dying.

Be agile and flexible to change. Learn to love learning and embrace change. It will happen.
How will you affect its direction?
